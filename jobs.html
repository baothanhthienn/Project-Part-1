AI/ML Engineer (Junior Level)

Location: Hawthorn

Job Type: Full-time

Department: Information Technology / Artificial Intelligence

Job Overview

We are seeking a talented Junior AI/ML Engineer to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will work on designing, developing, and deploying AI/ML solutions that address real-world business challenges.

Experience Level:

0-2 years

Salary:

$70,000 - $80,000 per year

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist in the development and implementation of machine learning models and AI solutions.
  • Preprocess and analyze structured and unstructured datasets.
  • Support the deployment of AI models into production environments.
  • Research and stay updated on new AI/ML technologies and methodologies.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand project requirements.
  • Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code.

Qualifications & Skills:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, or a related field.
  • Basic understanding of machine learning algorithms and deep learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn).
  • Proficiency in Python, R, or another relevant programming language.
  • Knowledge of data preprocessing techniques and tools.
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP) is a plus.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Good communication and teamwork abilities.

AI/ML Engineer (Senior Level)

Location: Hawthorn

Job Type: Full-time

Department: Information Technology / Artificial Intelligence

Job Overview

We are seeking a talented Senior AI/ML Engineer to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will lead the design, development, and deployment of AI/ML solutions that address real-world business challenges.

Experience Level:

5+ years

Salary:

$145,000 - $200,000 per year

Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead the design, development, and deployment of scalable AI/ML models.
  • Optimize and fine-tune machine learning algorithms for high performance.
  • Build and maintain data pipelines for large-scale datasets.
  • Develop and implement AI-driven solutions to improve business processes.
  • Provide technical guidance and mentorship to junior AI/ML engineers.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to define AI/ML strategies and roadmaps.
  • Ensure best practices in model explainability, security, and compliance.

Qualifications & Skills:

  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science
  • 5+ years in AI/ML roles, with expertise in deploying models at scale and leading teams
  • Experience in applying AI/ML to real-world problems in various industries.
  • Python, C++, Java, SQL.
  • Expertise in TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, and traditional ML algorithms
  • Strong in data preprocessing, feature engineering, and statistical analysis.
  • Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) and deployment tools like Docker, Kubernetes.
  • Familiarity with Spark, Hadoop for handling large datasets
  • Expertise in NLP, computer vision, or reinforcement learning.
  • Knowledge of MLOps, AI in production systems, and advanced mathematics.
